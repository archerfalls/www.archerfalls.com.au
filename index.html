Archer Falls is a private airfield located midway between Woodford and Kilcoy in the beautiful Sunshine Coast hinterland. We provide our friendly flyer visitors with an opportunity to escape their local circuit area and experience a relaxing flight area in a pristine bush environment.

“Archer Falls was developed by flyers, for flyers, for the good of flying”

So when you’re contemplating where to take your aircraft for the weekend, contact us and head out to Archer Falls to enjoy a fun filled weekend flying, bush walking and relaxing by the warmth of a fire as the picturesque sunsets over the airfield.
